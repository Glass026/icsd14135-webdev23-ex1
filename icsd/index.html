The University of the Aegean consists of six campuses spread across the Aegean Archipelago.

The Department of Information & Communication Systems Engineering is located in Karlovasi, Samos. This offers significant advantages for studying and everyday life, compared to other urban study environments. Boasting beautiful landscapes, sports teams and other activities, a low cost of living, all in a friendly and hospitable environment.
